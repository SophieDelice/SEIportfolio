About Me

Hi! I am Sophie, a software developer located near Boston, Massachusetts. Originally from Haiti. I completed a 24-week immersive boot camp with General Assembly in May 2023, where I found that I enjoy learning about Javascript, ExpressJS, Django, and NodeJS.

I continue learning by reading current tech articles on any new tech discovery. And by practicing and dedicating my time to completing coding challenges and achieving my goal of becoming a security software developer. I am passionate about cultivating software structures with the user in mind that focuses on their exposure and the safety of their digital products.

Outside of coding, you will find me with a book in one hand, and an ice coffee on the other. I love plants and use drawing as a source of creativity, self-expression, and a form of self-care.
